Who is Emma Chamblerain?

Emma - Gen Z queen of the internet

Emma Chamberlain is a content creator and YouTuber. Since starting her channel in 2017 shehas broken the creator mould and become a bona fide celebrity with over 16.1 million Instagram followers and 11.8 million Youtube subscribers. Her partnership with Louis Vuitton in 2019 was the first time a major luxury player teamed up with a YouTuber, a now common arrangement. Since then, she has been named a Louis Vuitton and Cartier ambassador, and has hosted Met Gala red carpet interviews for Vogue since 2021.
